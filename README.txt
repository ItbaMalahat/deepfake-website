Important limitation
Google Sites is not a normal hosting platform where a zip of HTML/CSS/JS can be uploaded and published as-is.
Google's official help shows that you can publish a Google Site and add content through Insert, Embed, or Full page embed, including HTML/CSS/JS blocks.
That means the site can be prepared for Google Sites, but it cannot be directly published to your Google account from here.

Best free route
1. Host the /site folder for free on GitHub Pages or Cloudflare Pages.
2. In Google Sites, either:
   - add buttons linking to those pages, or
   - use Full page embed for the hosted pages.
